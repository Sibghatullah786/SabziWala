ABOUT SABZIWALA

SabziWala is an online e-commerce shopping store where you can find fresh vegetables,fruits and dryfruits with minimal cost, best quality with free delivery on your doorstep. SabziWala started its business in 2010 in Abbottabad intending to reduce the gap in the Agri value chain by connecting farmers and end consumers, making sure that farmers get a fair price and our customers, top quality products. We initially started our bussiness as a store in Abbottabad where we were serving our customers by hand. Now we realize that due to busy routine and current Pandemic situation of Corona which has affected many people. We thought to start an online shopping service for our customers and we have launched a website to serve our customers. We are working on Android and IOS applications as well and InshAllah very soon, our applications will be available on Google Play Store and IPhone App Store. We have maintained a standard for delivery of fresh fruits and vegetables, straight from the shop, to your doorstep. Now our focus is on catering to the comfort and ease of our customers and ensuring they enjoy fresh fruits and vegetables. At SabziWala, from the sourcing of the product, its purchasing, packaging and delivery are all done in house. We supervise your order from start to finish, making quality checks and thorough inspections before we deliver with caution and experience to our customers. SabziWala not only operates online but also has a shop in Salhad Abbottabad, so that you may come to visit us and handpick the fruits and vegetables you desire. Our team is passionate and committed to our cause, that we can help farmers, and at the same time bring their farm-fresh goodness to our customer's kitchen.
